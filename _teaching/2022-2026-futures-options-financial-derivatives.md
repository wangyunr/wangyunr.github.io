This is a teaching assistant experience for a course on AEM 4210/6410 Futures, Options and Financial Derivatives. The course covered derivatives markets, hedging strategies, options risk management, and applied commodity-market analysis.

Responsibilities
======

Helped design market-based practice problems linking futures spreads, geopolitical risk premiums, and price volatility to energy spread-trading strategies across crude oil and natural gas markets.

Applied Commodity-Market Case Studies
======

Assisted in developing applied commodity-market case studies using real futures and spot price data to analyze how supply-demand shocks, basis movements, and inventory conditions affect hedging decisions and commodity price-risk management across metals, grains, and dairy markets.

Options and Derivatives Risk Management
======

Instructed students on options and derivatives risk management, guiding them through strategy payoffs, the Greeks, Delta hedging, and building Delta-Vega neutral portfolios using Excel-based modeling.
